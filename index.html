DCS Typing Practice

Welcome to DCS Typing Practice!

This tool is designed to help students practice typing with one-to-one support. Enter the text you'd like your student to type. The program will:

  • Speak each letter, symbol, or word aloud
  • Highlight the correct key on an onscreen keyboard
  • Prevent incorrect keystrokes
  • Give instant feedback when an incorrect key is pressed
  • Celebrate success with confetti and applause
  • Display typing accuracy and WPM at the end
