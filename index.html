Welcome to DCS Typing Practice

This tool is designed to provide structured, error-free typing practice for students. It’s especially helpful for students who benefit from visual key prompts, audio support, and one-key-at-a-time learning — modeled after the Just My Type program.

To use:
  1. Click “Start” below to begin.
  2. Type the sentence, phrase, or passage you want the student to practice.
  3. Click “Start Typing”.
  4. The student will retype the text with support and feedback after each keystroke.

When finished, WPM and accuracy will display, and a summary can be printed.
